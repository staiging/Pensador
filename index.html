Mezcal is the product of terroir – the earth, climate, biology and inherited techniques of a region. In the mountains and valleys of Oaxaca each region is distinct, giving mezcals unique local identities. El Camino Del Pensador is the flavour of Miahuatlán: defined by the place, process and people of the communities which branch from the Calle Pensamientos - Thoughts Road. It is earthy, aromatic, and mineral rich. Produced in tiny batches with great care, drinkers are encouraged to put the same time and consideration into each sip.

“I was born here and I never left. I worked and dedicated entirely to this. The more time you put in, the better the product comes out.”

ATENÓGENES GARCÍA MAESTRO MEZCALERO
